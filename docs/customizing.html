The N.A. Cleantime Calculator (NACC)

Customized NACC

NACC was designed to be modified. You can do all of the modifications through CSS. We provide three default theme files, but you can easily create your own.

Here's a manifest of the styles that are overloaded in the default theme files (NOTE: These are all low-specificity overloads):

  • div.nacc_main
    This is the main surrounding "box" of the calculator.
  • div.nacc_results
    This is the area in which both the textual results and the keytags are displayed.
  • div.nacc_msg
    This is where the textual results are displayed. This is not displayed until you press "Calculate."
  • div.nacc_keytags
    This is where the keytags are displayed. This is not displayed until you press "Calculate."
  • span.nacc_title
    This is the overall title that is displayed at the top of the main section.
  • span.nacc_prompt
    This is the prompt, asking you to enter your cleandate.
  • a.nacc_reset_link
    This is displayed in the main area to reset the cleandate to today.
  • a.nacc_change_link
    This is the link displayed in the keytags area to change the layout.

There are a great many more classes and IDs that you can use in your customization. Our three defaults are meant to illustrate a very simple modification.

Feel free to explore the implementation, but remember that most of the calculator is generated dynamically, as direct DOM construction. This means that you can't just peek at the page source to see what the code looks like.
